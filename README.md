Hands-on Engineering Manager | Bringing AI-Based Products to Production at Scale.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I am an engineering leader with over two decades of professional experience, marked by significant achievements. My enthusiasm for technology and continuous learning has led me to significant tech and managerial roles in the financing, retail, and, ultimately health industries. 

I hold a Master's in Software Engineering and a Bachelor's in Computer Science, which have laid a solid foundation for my career. My involvement in a wide array of projects has not only refined my technical skills but also led me to senior positions such as Software Architect, Principal Engineer, and currently Director of Engineering. My particular interest in Machine Learning and AI has been a driving force in my career, especially in making complex models practical for real-world applications. 

A major highlight of my career was the successful sale of my first startup (in 2016), Boolabs, to B2W Digital, the largest e-commerce platform in Latin America. Another significant achievement was my contribution as the main contributor of Marvin IA, an innovative MLOps platform incubated by the Apache Foundation, now widely used by companies and data scientists around the globe.
